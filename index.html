Scenario 1: The Suspicious Email Situation:

You receive an email from what appears to be your school’s IT department. The email states that your account will be locked unless you click a link to reset your password immediately. The email address looks almost correct but has a slight misspelling.

Discussion Questions:
  • What should you do before clicking the link?
  • How can you verify if the email is legitimate?
  • Why might hackers want you to act quickly without verifying?
